Water Restaurant

Water Restaurant

We constructed a water restaurant in August of 2016 as steel structure according to the requirement of Waterfront Hotel. Its specifications are 16 meters long, 16 meters wide and total displacement of 80 tons, the movable water restaurant designed with no-wall that holds 10 dining tables and 60 people can be surrounded by small yacht in order for the client to view around and ensuring a cool dining environment, the roof was built with double-layer steel in view of the heat of sunshine & rain, and the internal decoration style can be arranged according to the requirement of client to provide consumer with the perfect dining environment, it certainly brings substantial benefits to the owner as well
