A tugboat is used for towing the ship without self-propelled ability and rescuing the oceanic ships in danger. There is no any cargo cabin in tugboat that is with high-power propulsion host and towing equipment, its feature is of "small body, big strength". When a tugboat tows a barge(s), it can perform as a train tows its carriages in one-row
